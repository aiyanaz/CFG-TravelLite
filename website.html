Life's a beach in Cornwall



Join us on England's rugged, southwestern tip for a surf and sandy toes! There's more to Cornwall than meets the eye and we're confident you'll leave it with a soft spot in your heart.

'Crab' your bags for a weekend in Cromer



A traditional seaside resort, this quaint town in Norfolk is perfect for those looking to escape and relax. Small but mighty, the town boasts panoramic views of the North Sea coastline and is home to a famous pier ­and Pavilion Theatre.

Touch the 'Skye' in Scotland



Renowned for its rolling hills and breathtaking vistas, the Isle of Skye is a dream come true for avid hikers and photographers. It truly is the perfect place to unwind and disconnect for a few days.
